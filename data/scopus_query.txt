# Scopus Search Query

**Execution date:** February 14, 2026  
**Database:** Scopus (Elsevier)  
**Results:** 2,425 peer-reviewed journal articles

## Complete Query String

```
TITLE-ABS-KEY((PM10 OR PM2.5 OR "particulate matter") 
AND (forecast* OR predict* OR "time series" OR "machine learning" 
     OR "deep learning" OR ARIMA OR LSTM) 
AND ("air quality" OR "air pollution"))
AND PUBYEAR > 1999 AND PUBYEAR < 2027
AND DOCTYPE(ar) AND LANGUAGE(English)
```

## Query Components

1. **Pollutant specification:**  
   `PM10 OR PM2.5 OR "particulate matter"`

2. **Forecasting task:**  
   `forecast* OR predict* OR "time series" OR "machine learning" OR "deep learning" OR ARIMA OR LSTM`

3. **Domain:**  
   `"air quality" OR "air pollution"`

4. **Temporal scope:**  
   `PUBYEAR > 1999 AND PUBYEAR < 2027`

5. **Document filters:**  
   `DOCTYPE(ar)` — peer-reviewed journal articles only  
   `LANGUAGE(English)` — English-language publications only

## Export Instructions

To reproduce the corpus:

1. Go to scopus.com
2. Paste the complete query string into Advanced Search
3. Click "Search"
4. Verify result count (should be 2,425 ± small variation due to indexing updates)
5. Select all results → Export → CSV format
6. Include fields: EID, Title, Abstract, Year, Authors, Source, DOI, Cited by count
7. Save as `scopus_export_raw.csv`
